REASON FOR ITS EXISTENCE:

To help eradicate the social problems, to create sustainable connections among members of our society&  using modern views and knowledge
to drive the whole society towards socio-economically and politically healthy and prosperous state

Our Focal Points:

How to create rooms for socio-economic independence of youths through innovation at local and state levels?

BASTARA SAMAJ MOMENTS

We like to cherish the every moment of success as a tribute to our society while at the same time embrace the challenge society is currently facing without hesitation to mitigate/solve it. We are always happy to cooperate with local people and governments of various levels in doing so.

We promise that we will lead our Samaj through ups and downs providing our society unparalleled socio-economic service at every interaction. Whether it's about solving unemployment problems,reducing unhealthy pollitical rivalry, securing funding for innovations, increasing local people's engagement, or boosting connections, we develop a suite of society-tailored services to fulfill your expectations and realize them within suitable timeline.
